Fish. Then we found the most popular fish in most restaurants is Salmon. So we went with it. Now, we have Salmon Cookies.

What are Salmon Cookies?

Salmon Cookies are exactly what they sound - cookies made with Salmon. Everybody knows chocolate chip cookies, oatmeal raisin, sugar cookies. They all get their sweetness from something. Well Salmon is sweet too. So... why not?

Sure, we created it as a joke at first. But we had somebody on our team determined to make them taste good. She already enjoyed baking, she knew I (among others) loved cookies. So we went with it. Here we are, 7 months later, and we made some dang good cookies.

Starting off just as a normal cookie, you get flour, eggs, butter, baking soda, brown sugar, the works. The kicker is when you add the Salmon. The key to balance the Salmon flavor is molasses. Molasses is a good binder for the Salmon, gives the cookie a good color, and all around just good added flavor to really challenge your taste buds and what you think you know about food.

Then it comes to the cooking them. We like to make them into fish shapes, so we normally just bake an entire sheet of cookies at a time, then we have a big cutting sheet that has minimal waste and a bunch of mirrored fish. That way, we can create lots of fish shapes with a wonderful cookie, then frost them however!
